How to Stay Safe from Hackers

We are a collective of ethical minds united against digital threats.

Our mission is to spread awareness and defend users from online exploitation.

We value privacy and anonymity — your identity is your power.

Use strong, unique passwords for every account.

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.

Never click unknown or suspicious links.

Avoid public Wi-Fi without a trusted VPN.

Don’t trust emails asking for credentials or personal data.

Avoid plugging unknown USB drives into your system.

Keep your system and antivirus software updated.

Always log out after accessing your account on shared devices.
